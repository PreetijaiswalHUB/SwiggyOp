Our Services

Food Ordering

In these tough times of Covid we at SwiggyOP provides in restaurants-controlled online food ordering. the restaurants create their own website an app, or choose to hire a delivery vendor. if they choose to create their own website, they make sure to obtain software that manages the orders efficiently meaning it has the capability to manage different orders at once.

Food Catering

A customer can choose to have the food delivered or for pick-up. The process consist of a customer choosing the restaurant of their choice, scanning the menu items, choosing an item, and finally choosing for pick-up or delivery. Payment is then administrated by paying with a credit card or debit card through the app or website or in cash at the restaurant when going to pickup.

Bulk Ordering
